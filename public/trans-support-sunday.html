Trans Support Sunday Acupuncture

Trans Support Sunday Acupuncture is not a business, but a monthly day of service providing gender-queer folks with acupuncture for support and stress management. We are two Licensed Acupuncturists, Hilary Constant and Natasha Colburn, who have regular day jobs working independently at Form Health Clinic. On every second Sunday of the month we magically transform into volunteer relief providers for any transgender, non-binary, or gender- expansive members of our community who want some blissful, luscious, relaxing support.
